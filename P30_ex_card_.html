Bhopal, often called the City of Lakes, is the capital of Madhya Pradesh, India. The city's name is derived from the Upper Lake (Bhojtal), which was constructed in the 11th century by Raja Bhoj. Bhopal is known for its picturesque lakes, including the Upper Lake and Lower Lake, which are not only scenic but also serve as a vital source of drinking water for the city.
